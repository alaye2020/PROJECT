As one of the leading restaurant franchise groups in South Africa, we’re constantly looking to grow the Wimpy Family.


When you become a Wimpy franchisee, you can rest assured that we will provide you with all the training necessary to help you make a success of your business. However, success will ultimately depend on your hard work and commitment.


If you are a hands-on operator, willing to put in the daily attention required to ensure your Wimpy is profitable and meets the business requirements of our brand, then this is the opportunity for you. You’ll need to love working with people, as pleasing customers will be the key to success.


So, if you believe you have the entrepreneurial skills to run your own Wimpy and become part of a winning recipe, contact us.
