FRANCHISING

Three words govern our approach to preparing your favourites: Local is lekker. From our made-to-order coffee to our locally-sourced dairy, there’s always plenty of Mzansi mnandi to enjoy at Wimpy. We are also constantly thinking of ways to reduce, recycle, and reinvent our food packaging. That’s why we don’t just serve food, we serve food with thought.

Food safety

At Wimpy, the safety and well-being of our customers is our top priority. We’re committed to serving our customers the great tasting, high quality meals they expect from us, every time, making sure that the food we serve is always delicious and safe to eat.

The food we serve is fresh, and adheres to the very highest requirements, using quality ingredients while meeting regulatory requirements and international best practice standards. All Wimpy restaurants are required to uphold our stringent food safety standards in order to make sure we serve fresh and quality meals. Wimpy restaurants are audited to make sure that all health and safety requirements are upheld.

Our suppliers are also required to adhere to our demanding food safety standards and are independently audited to make sure food safety requirements are maintained at all times.

We take complaints related to our food very seriously and strive to respond to potential health and safety issues related to the food we serve as a priority. It’s important to us that our customers have a great experience at our restaurants and serving you a fresh, tasty and quality meal will always come first and foremost!

Special dietary requirements

We always go the extra mile to make sure there’s a seat for everyone at our table. Let us know if you have any dietary restrictions, food allergies, intolerances, or lifestyle choices; and we’ll find a meal to suit your needs.

Nutritional Info

We've included the kilojoule count and allergen information of each food & beverage item we serve on our menu. This information together with the full nutritional breakdown can be found next to each menu item on our website to help you make more informed choices when you visit us.

We aim to ensure that our food is sourced, produced, and grown in a responsible manner, which is why all the eggs we use to make our lekker brekkies are free range.

Sustainable packaging

Given our commitment to packaging that doesn't harm the environment, we’ve launched the Wimpy Eco Cup, which is 100% compostable, biodegradable and recyclable . We’re also committed to only using paper straws in our restaurants, and have removed polystyrene containers from our restaurants.

Given our commitment to packaging that doesn't harm the environment, we’ve launched the Wimpy Eco Cup, which is 100% compostable, biodegradable and recyclable. We’re also committed to only using paper straws in our restaurants, and have removed polystyrene containers from our restaurants.

But that’s not the only step we’ve taken to make our packaging as eco-friendly as possible. Noticed something different about the packaging on our Refresher Towels? We’ve made the switch from plastic to laminated paper foil packaging, allowing us to reduce our plastic footprint by 38%!

Environment management

We continually improve the eco-efficiency of our operations to reduce our impact on the environment. We’ve done away with the wrapper on the complementary biscuit that comes with our coffees. This alone is expected to reduce our plastic footprint by 4 tons a year.

We support local

At Wimpy, we don’t just talk local; we source local, too. Our famous chips are made from proudly South African Herta & Van Der Plank cultivar potatoes, sourced from the Sandveld region. In addition, all our dairy is sourced from the Dairy Group, a manuafacturer that’s equally as passionate about sustainability as they are about uplifting local farmers. As for our coffee beans? They’re roasted right here in Mzansi. Even our menu is local and is available in all 11 official languages.

Community isn’t confined to Mzansi’s borders. We’ve developed long-lasting relationships with business partners who are serious about sustainability. Our 100% unadultered honey is provided by Musanya Honey Co; a company that aims to bring meaningful development to rural Zambia by working with local chiefs to give communities control of the honey production in their surroundings. Musanya Honey Co sources its honey from Miombo Woodlands, an ecoregion untouched by modern agriculture, pollution, pesticides or chemicals.

From a product perspective

We understand that our customers have diverse dietary needs, which is why we also serve vegetarian and vegan offerings, as well as low-carb menu items. We also have several Halaal-certified restaurants in our stable, and Halaal-suitable alternatives at selected restaurants.

Reach for a Dream

For more than 30 years, Reach For A Dream has been fulfilling the dreams of children who have been diagnosed with a life-threatening illness. Since 2014, Wimpy has been a supporter of Slipper Day, which is Reach For A Dream's largest annual fundraiser. When Slipper Day was cancelled in 2020, because of lockdown restrictions, we created a donation portal on our website to keep on supporting this inspiring non-profit organisation.
