Hygiene Policy

Wimpy Hygiene Policy

  1. DO YOUR STAFF WEAR MASKS

      1. In line with the Department of Health’s announcement on 23 June 2022, the use of masks has been lifted and guests, as well as staff are not required to use face coverings. However, our restaurant staff are at liberty to wear them should they deem it fit.
      2. Should you see it fit to keep wearing your mask, you are welcome to do so.


  2. ARE CUSTOMERS REQUIRED TO PRACTISE SOCIAL DISTANCING?

      1. In line with the Department of Health’s latest guidelines, guests are not required to practise social distancing at our restaurants.


  3. IS HAND SANITISER STILL AVAILABLE FOR USE AT WIMPY?

      1. Alcohol-based hand sanitiser with at least 70% ethyl or isopropyl alcohol is available for staff at designated workstations and at the entrance for guests.


  4. WHICH OTHER MEASURES IS WIMPY TAKING TO PREVENT THE SPREAD OF COVID19?

      1. Staff members are screened for symptoms at the beginning and end of their shift. These records are kept and evaluated for signs of infection.
      2. Our restaurant employees continue to practice heightened hygiene standards including regular washing and sanitisation of their hands, working surfaces, and restaurant furniture after each use, as well as ensuring the restaurant’s general cleanliness is maintained.


  5. ARE THERE ANY RESTRICTIONS ON THE NUMBER OF GUESTS ALLOWED IN A RESTAURANT?

      1. There are currently no state-mandated caps on occupancy, so all our restaurants will allow as many patrons as comfortably possible.
