Discovery and Wimpy join forces to reward safe driving

Wimpy has partnered with Discovery Driving Insure to reward drivers for their good driving behaviour. Drivers insured with Discovery Business Insurance and on the Vitality Drive for Business programme will be able to earn weekly rewards, which they can now redeem at Wimpy on a selection of Wimpy favourites.

How it works

Vitality Drive for Business is a driver behaviour programme designed for businesses that uses telematics technology to measure and reward good driving. Every week, drivers who have driven more than 100kms with a driving score of 70 or more qualify for an airtime or data voucher, meal, coffee or smoothie from one of their partners.

These rewards can now also be redeemed at Wimpy to enjoy some of the classics, namely a Wimpy Burger, Streaky Breakfast or a Regular Famous Wimpy Coffee.

Discovery Insure Ltd is an authorised financial services provider. Registration number 2009/011882/06. Limits, maximum fuel and Gautrain spend limits, terms and conditions apply.
